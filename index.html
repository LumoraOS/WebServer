LumoraOS has been discontinued.

LumoraOS is no longer under active development and will most likely never return.

The main reason is that the system relies on a closed-source kernel, which makes further development extremely complex and time-consuming. Maintaining, updating, and expanding LumoraOS under these conditions is not realistically sustainable for the development team.

Status: Discontinued Kernel: Closed Source Future: Unlikely

Official Statement

LumoraOS started as an ambitious project with the goal of creating something unique and powerful. Over time, however, the technical limitations caused by the non-open-source kernel became a major barrier.

Continuing development would require a disproportionate amount of effort for relatively little progress. As a result, the developers have decided to stop working on the project indefinitely.

At this point, there are no plans for a rewrite, fork, or successor.

— LumoraOS Development Team

FAQ

Why was LumoraOS discontinued?
Due to the closed-source kernel and the excessive development effort required.

Will LumoraOS come back?
Very unlikely.

No, the kernel restrictions prevent that.

Not from the original developers.
